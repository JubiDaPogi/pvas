PVAS is an online appointment system built to make scheduling veterinary care simple for both pet owners and our clinic staff. Instead of calling in or waiting in line to book a visit, you can check real-time appointment availability on our calendar, pick an open date, and submit your pet's details online in just a few minutes.

Once your request is submitted, you'll receive a unique appointment code. Use it anytime on the Appointment page to look up your booking status, no account required. Behind the scenes, our staff reviews every request, confirms or reschedules as needed, and keeps daily appointment slots limited so every pet gets the attention it deserves.

We currently welcome dogs, cats, hamsters, rabbits, and birds, with services ranging from routine check-ups to vaccination, immunization, and anti-rabies shots. Visit Our Services to see what's available for your pet, or head straight to Appointment to reserve your slot today.
